# Privacy Policy - Budget Planner

**Last updated: May 22, 2026**

Budget Planner ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal finance app that helps you track income, expenses, budgets, bills, debts, savings goals, receipts, and AI-powered money insights. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and how you can control your data.

## Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address and any profile details you choose to add.

The app stores financial data you enter, including transactions, categories, budgets, savings goals, recurring bills, debts, imported CSV rows, receipt scan results, and related notes.

If you use premium features, subscription status is managed through RevenueCat, Apple App Store, or Google Play. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

If you use AI features, relevant financial context or receipt images may be sent to an AI provider so the feature can generate a response.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

- Sync your budget data across your devices.
- Show dashboards, charts, reminders, and financial insights.
- Import, export, categorize, and clean up transactions.
- Process receipt scans and AI Budget Coach requests.
- Verify premium access and manage subscriptions.
- Provide support when you contact us.

We do not sell your personal or financial data.

## Data Storage And Security

Budget Planner uses Supabase for authentication and database storage. Data is encrypted in transit. Row-level security is used so users can only access their own records.

Session data is stored locally on your device using secure storage where available.

## Third-Party Services

Budget Planner may use:

- Supabase for authentication and database services.
- RevenueCat for subscription management.
- Apple App Store and Google Play for purchases.
- Expo and EAS for app infrastructure and notifications.
- Google Gemini or OpenAI for AI features.

Each third-party service processes data under its own privacy policy.

## Notifications

If you enable reminders, Budget Planner may schedule notifications for bills, budgets, goals, payday reminders, or other financial reminders. You can disable notifications in the app or your device settings.

## Data Retention

We keep your account data while your account is active. If you delete your account in the app, Budget Planner removes your login and associated app data from our servers.

Some purchase history may remain with Apple, Google, or RevenueCat as required for subscription, refund, tax, fraud prevention, or legal recordkeeping.

## Your Choices And Rights

You can:

- Export your transaction data from the app.
- Edit or delete financial records in the app.
- Delete your account from Settings > Delete Account.
- Contact us for privacy questions or support.

## Children's Privacy

Budget Planner is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes To This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date.
