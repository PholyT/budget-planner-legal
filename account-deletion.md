# Account Deletion - Budget Planner

**Last updated: May 22, 2026**

Budget Planner lets you delete your account directly inside the app.

## Delete Your Account In The App

1. Open Budget Planner.
2. Sign in to your account.
3. Go to **Settings**.
4. Tap **Delete Account**.
5. Confirm the deletion prompt.

This permanently deletes your Budget Planner login and associated app data from our servers, including transactions, budgets, savings goals, bills, debts, referral records, promo access records, and AI usage history.

## What May Be Kept Outside Budget Planner

Apple App Store, Google Play, and RevenueCat may retain purchase or subscription records as required for billing, refunds, tax, fraud prevention, or legal obligations.
